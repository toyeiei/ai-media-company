# How to Do SEO in the Age of AI: A Practical Guide for 2026

SEO has changed dramatically. AI-powered search is rewriting the rules. Here's how to stay visible.

## The Old SEO is Dead

Keyword stuffing, link farms, and thin content — these tactics used to work. Not anymore. Search engines now understand intent, context, and quality better than any human optimizer.

## What AI Search Wants

Search engines in 2026 prioritize:
- **E-E-A-T** — Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trust
- **User intent** — Not just keywords, but why someone is searching
- **Original insight** — AI favors content that adds perspective, not repeats
- **Engagement signals** — Time on page, shares, bounce rate matter more

## How to Adapt

1. **Write for humans first.** AI can detect when you're writing for algorithms.
2. **Be specific and factual.** cite real sources, include data, back up claims.
3. **Update old content.** Fresh, accurate content beats stale posts every time.
4. **Build genuine authority.** Quality backlinks from real publications beat shortcuts.
5. **Use AI as a tool, not a shortcut.** AI can help research and draft — but genuine expertise must come from you.

## The Honest Truth

SEO in 2026 rewards substance, not tricks. The sites that will win are the ones that provide genuine value, stay current, and build real authority.

Write better content. That's the whole strategy.
