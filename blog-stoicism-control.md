# Stoicism in 2026: Focus on What You Can Control

Two thousand years ago, Epictetus was born a slave. He would later become one of history's most influential philosophers. His secret? Understanding the difference between what we control and what we don't.

## The Core Idea

Stoicism teaches a simple mental模型: **dichotomy of control**.

You control:
→ Your thoughts
→ Your actions
→ Your responses

You don't control:
→ Other people's opinions
→ The economy
→ What happens to you
→ The past

## Why This Matters Now

We stress about things we can't change. We rage at news we can't influence. We dwell on the past. All of it — wasted energy.

## The Stoic Practice

Ask yourself: "Is this within my control?" If no → let it go. If yes → act.

That's it. No overthinking. No spiraling. Just clarity.

## The Modern Stoic

You don't need to meditate for hours or read ancient texts. Just train yourself to spot what's actually in your power — and focus there.

Everything else is noise. Cut it out.
